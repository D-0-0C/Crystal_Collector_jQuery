Press any of the collect buttons to start!

❖At the start of the game round you will be given a sacred crystal number that you want to match by collecting a certain number of the four types of sacred crystals.
❖Each of the four sacred crystals below will have a random assigned value. By cllicking on each of the four "collect" buttons you will add a specific number to your total score.
❖You will win the game round by matching your total score to the sacred crystal number. You will lose if your total score is greater than the sacred crystal number.
❖The assigned value of each of the four sacred crystals will intially be hidden at the start of the game, until the "collect" button is pressed revealing its value.
❖Remember, at the start of each new game round the sacred crystal number will change and the assigned value of each of the four sacred crystals will be changed.
